How To Play

This is a remake of the classic arcade game, Frogger. Your aim is to get to the water(blue tiles) without running into the bugs. Pick up hearts to increase lives left and gems to boost score. To move the player around, use the arrow keys on PC and Mac or swipe up, down, left, or right on touch screen devices. Click continue to start or the timed mode button if you're up for a challenge.

*Challenge Accepted*

You have 2 minutes to score as much as possible. Good luck!
