How To Play

This is a remake of the classic arcade game, Frogger.

  • Your aim is to get to the water(blue tiles). Avoid bugs!
  • Pick up hearts to increase lives left, gems to boost score and stars for a time, life and score boost combo
  • Use arrow keys to move player around on laptops or swipe up, down, left or right on touch screen devices
Click continue to start or the timed mode button if you're up for a challenge.
